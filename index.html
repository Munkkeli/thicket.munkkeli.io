The Story of Brian Jr.

You are playing as young Brian Jr. Brian is a 12-year-old fifth grader boy. The day is cloudy and Brian’s school day has just come to an end when he suddenly gets a text message from his mother. “Brian love, I’m making cinnamon rolls come, home quick before they get cold!” Brian loving his mother's cinnamon rolls decides to take a shortcut to get home as quickly as possible. The road that he normally takes goes around a thick forest that Brian has never gone through before. The forest looks dark and spooky, but Brian’s love for cinnamon rolls takes over, and he decides to go through anyway.

You have to help him get through the Thicket.
